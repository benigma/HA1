Marketing Strategy For 2018-19

The Harrow BID Board has adopted a proposal from Pop Marketing/Savills which will see them continuing to deliver marketing services for the BID and the town centre for the year from April 2018.

The marketing strategy and action plan will be delivered by Karen Deas, in her role of Marketing & Communications Manager for the BID.

At the Board meeting, Karen confirmed her intention that this year should be bigger and better with a view to maintaining and improving engagement with members and providing a better shopping and visitor experience. The strategy proposes three ‘Seasons’ of marketing activity, being Summer, Halloween and Christmas to raise awareness for a month at a time, rather than relying on one-off events that revolve around the public being in the town on a specific day.

Karen brings a wealth of experience to Harrow, as she previously worked with the Liverpool city centre BID as well as managing marketing for other towns. Anybody that has met her will testify she is a bundle of energy and enthusiasm! Please feel free to send your marketing messages direct to Karen on karen.deas@ha1bid.co.uk.
