Creating a compelling destination

All our marketing activity – from the organisation of special, seasonal events to the creation of smaller scale promotions – is aimed at potential shoppers, demonstrating how much Harrow has to offer and giving them a compelling reason to visit. The cumulative effect will be to reinforce an attractive overall brand identity that encompasses both shopping centres plus the wider retail offering along in the BID area.

A regular calendar of events is helping to keep Harrow town centre in the headlines and encouraging increased footfall. Their success, however, depends on the enthusiasm and hands-on involvement of local businesses.
